About Me

I connect people, ideas, and cultures, sometimes with translation, sometimes with AI tools, and sometimes just with a good old MMgoy and a smile.

With a background in language and a Hong Kong mindset, I believe in translanguaging as a way of life. I share what I know (or think I know) to make things more accessible, especially for those navigating between languages, systems, and realities.

I develop and test language-related AI tools too. When they work, I shout, “Holland Genius Ah!” When they don’t… haiyaa, sor9ly - it works on my computer!

Whether it’s building workflows, creating teaching resources, or just figuring things out one bug at a time, I believe that sharing knowledge (even when it’s Holland difficult) can spark connection, empowerment, and maybe even a laugh.
